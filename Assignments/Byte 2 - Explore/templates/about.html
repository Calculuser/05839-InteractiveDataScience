Accountability: About This Data Set

This dataset is part of that called Household Heating Statistics by District in 2008, which can be found in the following link.

  • Household Heating Statistics by District in 2008

    • In this project byte, I used part of it. Specifically, I used data to study household heating in Pennsylvania and removed some unrelated columns. Although it may be a bit old, it still reveals some information about household heating in Pennsylvania.

    Where did this data come from?

    I found this dataset in Google Fusion Tables. Google imported it from the View of American Community Survey 2008, 1 year by 110th Congressional District, from U.S. Census Bureau.

    Who has access to it?

    This dataset is public, which means anyone on the Internet has the access to it.
